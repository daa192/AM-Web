How It Works

For Suppliers


1. You want to expand

You realize that you're company is spinning on the same, old clients and you want to expand to new markets. You decide that exporting your retreads is an interesting opportunity.



2. Get in touch

You get in touch with us and we introduce to you our services. RetreadSupply works as a global sales representative for retreaded tires manufacturers.



3. Exchange information

After deciding you want to work with us, you provide us with information about your products, operations, and goals.



4. Sign Agreement

After we exchange information and agree on reasonable terms, we draft and agreement which is signed by both parties.



5. Sell

Shortly after signing, you can expect your sales cycles to increase exponentially.

For Buyers


1. You want to sell more products

You realize that you're company dominates the current markets it attends and that diversifying your product portfolio is a feasable opportunity for you.



2. Get in touch

You get in touch with us and browse the products we represent. RetreadSupply sells virtually any type, size, and design of retread.



3. Tell us what you need

After browsing our products, tell us exactly what you need so we can better help you.



4. Get a quote

We'll give you a detailed quote, which you can go over with your coworkers and analyze with time.



5. Buy

After receiving the quote all we need is an escrow and we can start shipping as much as you want.
